K(n)-local and T(n)-local Ambidexterity

Together with Itamar Mor I am organising a seminar on Ambidexterity in Chromatic Homotopy Theory. The aim is to prove K(n)- and T(n)-local ambidexterity following Carmeli-Schlank-Yanovski, and then go through some applications in the new year. Here is the syllabus until the Christmas break.
The seminar will meet in the seminar room of the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in Bonn from 16:15-18 on Wednesdays (the last planned talk before the break will be on Wednesday the 18th of December 2024).
