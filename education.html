Undergraduate

I did my Bachelor's degree with Honours in mathematics at the University of Edinburgh.
I did my bachelors dissertation under the nurturing supervision of Agata Smoktunowicz. It's topic was algebraic deformation theory - largely inspired by the works of Murray Gerstenhaber. It's title ended up being Abelian Categories, Hochschild (Co)homology and Algebraic Deformations. As is evident from the title a lot of time was spent learning about some elementary ideas required to work with algebraic deformations.
